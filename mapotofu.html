Mapo Tofu

Return to Homepage

Description

This recipe is for Mapo Tofu. A delcious Chinese dish that is spicy and fragrent.

Ingredients:

  • Soft Tofu
  • Ground Pork
  • Fermented Bean Paste
  • Green Onions
  • Chinese Condiments

Steps

  1. You will need to marinate your ground pork with multiple spices, such as soy sauce, chinese cooking wine, sugar, and sesame oil.
  2. Slice your tofu into bite sized cubes.
  3. Get a pot going with some oil and throw in your ground pork and green onions. Sautee untill you see some color.
  4. Add your fermented bean paste (I usually do spicy), and cook for a little longer.
  5. Add water and stir.
  6. Add your tofu in a cook for a little.
  7. Create a cornstarch slurry and add to the pork/tofu/water mixture until it becomes a gravy like consistency.
