NEOPOLITAN PIZZA

Return to Homepage

Description

This recipe is for a Neopolitan style pizza. A very simple, yet delicious dish which has an emphasis on the flavors of the fresh ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Fresh Basil
  • Parmesean Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to the highest possible temperature it goes. 500-550 fahrenheit.
  2. First, you're going to mix flour and water to make the dough. Set that aside and let it rest
  3. Next, you're going to used canned San Marzano tomatoes to create your sauce, add any spices to taste, and a pinch of sugar to balance the acidity.
  4. Slice or pull your cheese apart and set it aside.
  5. Now take your rested dough and stretch it out into a circular shape.
  6. Ladel your sauce on and place your cheese with spaces in between. Top with basil leaves.
  7. Top with more fresh basil, glug of olive oil, and freshly grate some parmesean cheese.
