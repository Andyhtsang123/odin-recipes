Sushi

Return to Homepage

Description

You can make sushi yourself at home! All you need is fish, rice, seaweed, and a bamboo mat.

Ingredients

  • Fresh fish of your choice
  • Shortgrain rice
  • Seaweed (Nori) sheets
  • Sushi vinegar or vinegar powder
  • Any sauces you'd like: spicy mayo, wasabi, yuzu kosho, etc.

Steps

  1. Rinse your rice 2-3 times. Place in the rice cooker and let that cook until completion.
  2. Prepare your fish, slice it into the appropriate size for your sushi ie. cube, slices, chopped, etc.
  3. After your rice is cooked, pour the vinegar and fold it into the rice and let it cool.
  4. Once your rice is cooled you can use it to make your sushi. You can either make nigiri or hand rolls
  5. If making nigiri: mold a small block of rice and place a piece of sliced fish on top before molding together.
  6. If making a handroll: lay a piece of nori down and spread out some rice. Add your fish, slice or chopped, and then begin to roll it into a log. Using the bamboo mat you can further shape it and neaten it.
